How it works:
  1. Captures microphone input (voice + speaker audio)
  2. Captures speaker output (what's being played)
  3. Subtracts: Clean Voice = Microphone - Speaker
  4. Outputs only your voice to applications

Why it works:
  âœ“ PC controls both microphone and speaker
  âœ“ Audio mixing is linear and predictable
  âœ“ Speaker output is known in advance
  âœ“ Perfect mathematical subtraction possible

Latency: ~20-50ms (imperceptible for voice calls)
CPU Usage: ~5-15% on modern systems
Requirements: Python 3.7+, Virtual audio device (VoiceMeeter)
