TROUBLESHOOTING:
â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•

Q: Still getting error?
A: Read the error message! It says which device is wrong.
   Try a different device from the list above.

Q: Still stuck?
A: Run: python list_devices.py
   Pick a MICROPHONE from input devices
   Pick a LOOPBACK from output devices (has "Voicemeeter" or "Stereo Mix")
   Pick SPEAKERS from output devices

Q: No audio output?
A: Make sure Discord/Teams has the OUTPUT device selected as microphone

Q: Speaker audio still comes through?
A: Increase "Subtraction Strength" slider to 1.5 or 2.0

Q: Your voice very quiet?
A: Decrease "Subtraction Strength" slider to 0.8

Q: Can't find loopback?
A: VoiceMeeter might not be installed properly
   Reinstall from: https://vb-audio.com/Voicemeeter/
   Then restart your PC
