WHAT IT DOES
================================================================================

For gamers using speakers instead of headsets:

1. Removes game/speaker audio from microphone input
2. Transmits ONLY your voice to Discord/Team Chat
3. You DON'T hear your own voice (no echo)
4. Teammates hear clean voice without game audio

Perfect for competitive gaming with team communication!
