================================================================================
                    VOICE ISOLATOR - QUICK REFERENCE CARD
================================================================================

WHAT IT DOES:
  Removes speaker output from microphone input
  Only your voice is transmitted to Discord/Teams/Zoom

HOW TO RUN:
  Option 1: Double-click run.bat
  Option 2: python voice_isolator.py

DEVICE SELECTION (3 STEPS):
  1. Microphone: Pick a real microphone (not Voicemeeter)
  2. Speaker Loopback: Pick Voicemeeter In 1 or Stereo Mix
  3. Output: Pick any Speakers device

BEST SETTINGS FOR YOUR SYSTEM:
  Microphone: [1] Microphone (Sound Blaster Audigy 5/Rx)
  Loopback:   [15] Voicemeeter In 1 (VB-Audio...)
  Output:     [6] Speakers (Audioengine HD3)

THEN CLICK: "Start Voice Isolation"

SLIDERS:
  Subtraction Strength: 0.5 to 2.0 (default 1.0)
    - Higher = removes more speaker audio
    - Lower = keeps more of your voice
  
  Delay Compensation: 0 to 1000 (default 0)
    - For timing adjustment if needed

SETUP IN YOUR APP:
  Discord:  Settings → Voice & Video → Input Device → Output device
  Teams:    Settings → Devices → Microphone → Output device
  Zoom:     Settings → Audio → Microphone → Output device

TEST:
  1. Play YouTube
  2. Speak into mic
  3. Have friend check on Discord
  4. Only your voice = SUCCESS

TROUBLESHOOTING:
  Error at start?
    → Check all 3 devices are selected
    → Try different device combinations
  
  Speaker audio still plays?
    → Increase Subtraction Strength slider to 1.5-2.0
  
  Your voice is quiet?
    → Decrease Subtraction Strength slider to 0.5-0.8
  
  Can't find loopback device?
    → VoiceMeeter is not installed
    → Download: https://vb-audio.com/Voicemeeter/

STATUS INDICATORS:
  GREEN light = Running (working)
  RED light = Stopped or Error

FILES YOU NEED:
  ✓ voice_isolator.py (main app)
  ✓ run.bat (launcher)
  ✓ requirements.txt (already installed)

HELP:
  Read: FIX_AND_RUN.txt for this session
  Read: QUICK_START.md for full setup
  Read: SETUP.md for detailed help
  Run: python list_devices.py to see devices

SUCCESS = Clean voice, no speaker audio!
