KEY INSIGHT:
â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•

VoiceMeeter is the "loopback" device you were looking for!

It's NOT a physical device that captures sound.
It's a SOFTWARE router that:
  1. Receives audio from your apps
  2. Sends it to your speakers
  3. Can be captured as INPUT by Voice Isolator

So when Voice Isolator captures from "Voicemeeter In 1", it's reading
whatever is FLOWING THROUGH VoiceMeeter - which is everything your
apps are playing!

This is PERFECT for voice isolation.
